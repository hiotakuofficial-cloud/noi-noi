Synopsis

Gol D. Roger was known as the "Pirate King," the strongest and most infamous being to have sailed the Grand Line. The capture and execution of Roger by the World Government brought a change throughout the world. His last words before his death revealed the existence of the greatest treasure in the world, One Piece. It was this revelation that brought about the Grand Age of Pirates, men who dreamed of finding One Piece—which promises an unlimited amount of riches and fame—and quite possibly the pinnacle of glory and the title of the Pirate King.

Enter Monkey D. Luffy, a 17-year-old boy who defies your standard definition of a pirate. Rather than the popular persona of a wicked, hardened, toothless pirate ransacking villages for fun, Luffy's reason for being a pirate is one of pure wonder: the thought of an exciting adventure that leads him to intriguing people and ultimately, the promised treasure. Following in the footsteps of his childhood hero, Luffy and his crew travel across the Grand Line, experiencing crazy adventures, unveiling dark mysteries and battling strong enemies, all in order to reach the most coveted of all fortunes—One Piece.

Main Characters

Monkey D. Luffy

Captain · Straw Hat Pirates

The protagonist who ate the Gum-Gum Fruit, giving him rubber powers. Dreams of becoming the Pirate King. Known for his straw hat given by Shanks and his unwavering determination.

Roronoa Zoro

Swordsman · First Mate

Master of the Three-Sword Style. Dreams of becoming the world's greatest swordsman. Extremely loyal to Luffy and possesses incredible strength and willpower.

Nami

Navigator · Cartographer

Expert navigator with an innate ability to predict weather. Dreams of drawing a complete map of the world. Skilled thief with a love for money and tangerines.

Sanji

Cook · Black Leg

Master chef and martial artist who fights using only his legs. Dreams of finding the All Blue, a legendary sea. Known for his chivalry and exceptional cooking skills.

Tony Tony Chopper

Doctor · Reindeer

A reindeer who ate the Human-Human Fruit. Dreams of curing all diseases. Serves as the crew's doctor with his medical expertise and transformative abilities.

Nico Robin

Archaeologist · Devil Child

Archaeologist who can read Poneglyphs. Dreams of discovering the true history of the world. Ate the Flower-Flower Fruit, allowing her to sprout body parts anywhere.

Franky

Shipwright · Cyborg

Cyborg shipwright who built the Thousand Sunny. Dreams of building a ship that sails around the world. Modified his own body with various weapons and gadgets.

Brook

Musician · Soul King

Skeleton musician who ate the Revive-Revive Fruit. Dreams of reuniting with Laboon. Provides entertainment and comic relief while being a skilled swordsman.

Jinbe

Helmsman · Fish-Man

Former Warlord and fish-man karate master. Dreams of coexistence between humans and fish-men. Serves as the crew's helmsman with unmatched aquatic abilities.

01

Information We Collect

To deliver and continuously improve the Hiotaku experience, we collect a limited set of information from our users. This includes:

Account information provided during registration, such as your name and email address.
Usage data, including which content you watch, search, and interact with on the platform.
Communication logs when you contact us through the in-app support system.
Device and technical information needed to deliver and optimise streaming quality.
02

How We Use Information

The information we collect is used exclusively to make Hiotaku work better for you. Specifically, your data is used for:

Operating and maintaining the platform and all its features.
Providing user support and resolving technical issues.
Generating personalised content recommendations based on your viewing history.
Improving platform performance, features, and overall user experience.

"We do not use your data for advertising purposes. Hiotaku has no ads and no advertising partners."

03

Information Sharing

Hiotaku does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party. We do not share your information for commercial gain.

In limited circumstances, data may be shared with trusted service providers who assist in operating the platform — strictly for technical and operational purposes, under confidentiality obligations. We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users.

04

Data Security

We take the protection of your information seriously. Hiotaku implements industry-standard security measures — including encryption in transit and at rest — to safeguard your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

While we do everything reasonable to protect your data, no system connected to the internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure. We encourage users to use strong, unique passwords and to report any suspected security issues through the in-app support system immediately.

05

Your Rights

You have full control over your personal data on Hiotaku. Through your account settings, you can:

Access the personal information stored against your account.
Update or correct any inaccurate account details at any time.
Delete your account and associated data permanently.
Request a copy of your data or raise any data-related concern through support.
06

Third-Party Services

Hiotaku integrates a small number of trusted third-party services to support platform infrastructure. Each of these operates under their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review independently.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or how your personal data is handled, please reach out through the in-app support system. All privacy-related inquiries are handled directly by the developer.

For privacy concerns, the in-app support system is the fastest and most direct way to reach us.
